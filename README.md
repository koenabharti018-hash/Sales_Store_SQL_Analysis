# Sales_Store_SQL_Analysis\

Overview
This project analyzes retail sales transaction data using SQL to generate actionable business insights. The dataset includes customer details, product information, order timestamps, payment modes, and order status.

The goal is to solve key business problems related to revenue optimization, customer behavior, and operational efficiency through structured data analysis.

## Business Problem 
The retail business lacks visibility into:
Sales performance trends
Customer purchasing patterns
Product profitability
Order and delivery inefficiencies
## This results in missed revenue opportunities and poor decision-making.

## Objectives
Identify top-performing products and categories
Analyze customer spending behavior
Detect peak order times
Evaluate order success, delays, and cancellations
Understand payment method preferences

## Tools & Technologies
SQL (SQL Server Management Studio 21)

## Data Analysis
Business Intelligence Concepts
Key Analysis & SQL Queries
1. Sales Performance
Revenue by category
Top-selling products
2. Customer Analysis
Top customers by spending
Purchase frequency
3. Product Insights
High revenue vs low demand products
4. Time Analysis
Peak hours and days
5. Order Performance
Delivery vs cancellation rates
6. Payment Insights
Most used payment methods - Credit Card

## Sample Insights
Identified top 20% products contributing to majority of revenue
Peak sales observed during evening hours
Certain payment modes showed higher failure/cancellation rates
Repeat customers contributed significantly to total sales

## Business Impact
This analysis helps:
Improve inventory planning
Optimize staffing during peak hours
Enhance customer experience
Increase revenue through data-driven decisions
